FAQ

Common questions, answered.

Can I bring my own golf clubs?
+
Absolutely. You're welcome to bring your own clubs with no restrictions or fees. Simply arrive, check in, and head to the first tee.
How do I hire clubs?
+
Club hire is $10 per full set per round. Add this when booking online or request at reception on arrival. We recommend booking ahead on weekends.
Do I need to book or can I walk in?
+
Walk-ins are welcome on weekdays. On weekends we strongly recommend booking 2–3 days in advance. Online booking takes under 2 minutes.
What's the difference between the trolley and the buggy?
+
The trolley ($5) is a push/pull cart — you walk and it carries your bag. The buggy ($25) is a drivable electric cart that takes you and your clubs around the full course.
How long does a round take?
+
9 holes takes approximately 1.5–2 hours. 18 holes takes around 3.5–4.5 hours depending on group size. Please allow enough time before the course closes at 7pm.
Do I need to be an experienced golfer?
+
Not at all. Boomerang Golf welcomes complete beginners through to experienced players. No handicap required. We're here for everyone.
